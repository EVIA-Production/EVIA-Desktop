EVIA Permissions Setup

To use EVIA with both microphone and system audio capture, you need to grant the necessary permissions. Follow the steps below:

Step 1: Microphone Access

EVIA needs access to your microphone to capture your voice.

Step 2: System Audio Capture

To capture system audio, macOS requires Screen Recording permission for both the main app and the helper.

In the settings window that opens:

  1. Click the lock icon in the bottom left to make changes (if needed)
  2. Click the "+" button
  3. Navigate to /Applications/Electron.app and add it
  4. Run the helper test below to trigger its permission request

Step 3: Verify Permissions

Step 4: Launch EVIA

Once all permissions are granted, you can launch the main application.
